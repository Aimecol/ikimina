Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the Ikimina platform

How do I get started with Ikimina?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, choose your plan, and either create a new group or join an existing one using a group code. Our onboarding process will guide you through setting up your group's bylaws and inviting members.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes, absolutely. We use bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS) to protect all data transmission and storage. Your financial information is encrypted and stored securely, and we comply with international data protection standards.

Can I integrate with mobile money services?

Yes, we support integration with major mobile money services including MTN MoMo and Airtel Money. This allows members to make contributions directly through their mobile wallets for added convenience.

What happens if I need to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time. We provide data export options so you can download all your group's financial records and member information before cancellation. No long-term contracts required.

Do you provide training for new users?

Yes! We offer comprehensive training including video tutorials, documentation, and live training sessions for Professional and Enterprise plans. Our support team is also available to help with any questions.

Can I manage multiple groups with one account?

Yes, with our Enterprise plan you can manage multiple Ikimina groups from a single account. This is perfect for organizations that oversee several community savings groups or cooperatives.
