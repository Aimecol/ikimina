Executive Biographies

Jean Claude Nzeyimana, Chief Executive Officer

Jean Claude brings over 15 years of experience in financial technology and community development to his role as CEO of Ikimina Digital Platform. Prior to founding the company, he served as Director of Digital Financial Services at a leading Rwandan bank and has been instrumental in several financial inclusion initiatives across East Africa.

He holds an MBA in Finance from the University of Rwanda and is a certified financial risk manager. Jean Claude is passionate about leveraging technology to preserve and enhance traditional community financial practices.

Download Full Bio

Marie Uwimana, Chief Technology Officer

Marie is a seasoned software engineer with over 12 years of experience building scalable financial applications. She leads the technical vision and development of the Ikimina Digital Platform, ensuring security, reliability, and user-friendly design.

She holds a Master's degree in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University and has previously worked with fintech companies in Silicon Valley. Marie is committed to creating technology solutions that are accessible to all users, regardless of their technical background.
