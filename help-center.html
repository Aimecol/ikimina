Frequently Asked Questions

The Ikimina Digital Platform is a comprehensive web-based management system designed specifically for community-based savings and lending groups (Ikimina/Ikibina) in Rwanda. It digitizes traditional group financial operations, providing modern tools for member management, financial tracking, loan processing, and meeting coordination.

The platform is designed for community savings groups in Rwanda, including group administrators, treasurers, secretaries, auditors, and members. Each role has specific permissions and access levels tailored to their responsibilities within the group.

Yes, the Ikimina Digital Platform is free for community-based savings groups. We believe in supporting financial inclusion and empowering Rwandan communities through accessible digital tools.

Account creation is managed by your group administrator. Contact your group leader to request membership. They will create your account and assign the appropriate role based on your position in the group.

Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact your group administrator.

The platform supports Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money), bank transfers, and cash payments. Your treasurer will verify and record all payments in the system.

Log into your member dashboard and navigate to the Contributions section. Select "Make Payment," choose your payment method, enter the amount, and follow the instructions. Your payment will be verified by the treasurer.

Go to the Loans section in your member dashboard and click "Apply for Loan." Fill out the application form with the loan amount, purpose, and repayment plan. Your application will be reviewed by the group leadership.

Loan eligibility is typically based on your contribution history, membership duration, and group policies. Generally, you must be an active member with regular contributions and no outstanding loan defaults.

Yes, we implement bank-level security measures including data encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits. Your personal and financial information is protected according to international security standards.

Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works on all modern mobile browsers. Simply visit the platform URL on your mobile device and log in with your credentials.
